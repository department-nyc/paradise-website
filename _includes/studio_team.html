— Who We Are
Luke Flynn Director of Brand

Luke is our brand specialist directing everything from the cultivation of a concept to the delivery of a website or campaign. Luke brought the knowledge he gathered working as Art Director for First & Last Design and as a designer for NY based agency Common Space.

Mark Hosny Business Director

Mark is the key driver and advisor of business growth and development for Paradise on all internal and client based initiatives. As the CFO and COO of a fast growing technology solutions company he brings 12 years of experience managing teams through times of extreme growth.

Jay O'Neill Director of Product

Jay spent more than 10 years on product design and management teams at action sports and lifestyle brands RVCA, Nixon, Billabong, Tsovet and RAEN before starting a freelance consultancy and becoming a partner in Leader Bag Co. where he is currently the Director of Product and Design. He co-founded Paradise in 2014.

Yuji Tomita Development Partner

Yuji manages all things digital and development at Paradise. He started a winery in Argentina and helped launch Grovemade, a wood products company based in Portland. He co-founded Paradise in 2014.
