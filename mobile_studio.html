Welcome to Paradise.

One of the most widely accepted interpretations of Paradise is the Garden of Eden, home to Adam and Eve before they got kicked out for eating fruit.

But there are as many notions of Paradise as there are people dreaming of it. For some, Paradise is a hammock swaying between palms on a deserted beach; for others, Paradise is an ice-cold beer at 5:15 on a Friday, or dinner and a movie–minus the kids. Some people even find Paradise while being struck with a pizza shovel in the basement of a nightclub.

For us, Paradise is our design and product studio. That’s what we called it– Paradise. And it happens to be our source of literal Paradise because it’s here that we’re able to take our knowledge and past experience working in-house for brands, and use it to create hard hitting and award winning new work for others. Our background sets us apart: we’ve been in the trenches and we know our shit. That’s Paradise.

— What Makes Us Different

In the Trenches. Our time spent in-house has given us insights into the customer feedback loop that many agencies never get access to. Launching award winning product lines with some of the most innovative marketing, sales, design and operations teams has taught us the complex balancing act of brand and business

New York City + Sydney. Travel is a key part of how we operate and the brands and products we create reflect that. And offices on directly opposite sides of the world means our team literally works around the clock.

Skin In The Game. We've built our own brands from the ground up. And we’re still doing it. We've had successes, but more importantly failures. We’ve bounced back bigger and badder than ever. We feel your pain and understand the ever-changing landscape because we live and learn in it everyday.

— Who We Are
Jay O'Neill Director of Product

Jay spent more than 10 years on product design and management teams at action sports and lifestyle brands RVCA, Nixon, Billabong, Tsovet and RAEN before starting a freelance consultancy and becoming a partner in Leader Bag Co. where he is currently the Director of Product and Design. He co-founded Paradise in 2014.

Luke Flynn Creative Director

In 2013 Luke used the knowledge he gathered working as Art Director for Saturdays NYC and designing for NY based agencies Imprint Projects and Common Space — to co-found the accessories company Prize NYC in 2014. He co-founded Paradise in 2014.

Margaux Le Pierrès Designer

Margaux balances out the in-house work with a strong agency background and a focus on branding and digital design. Margaux has been recognized by D&AD, The Type Directors Club and Awwwards for her excellence in digital design.

Yuji Tomita Development Partner

Yuji manages all things digital and development at Paradise. He started a winery in Argentina and helped launch Grovemade, a wood products company based in Portland. He co-founded Paradise in 2014.

— How We Do It

We Discover. We look at the big picture and how your brand fits into it. Where are you now and where do you want to be? You know your brand best–tell us about it. We listen. We nod. We hold our chins. We remove our glasses and suck on the stem. Then we blow your ever-lovin’ socks off.

We Strategize. We take the big questions into consideration: how will you stand out? Who are you? Why does your company exist? Why do you exist? Do you exist? Is anything real? Where am I? Then we blow your ever-lovin’ socks off a second time.

We Play. All the best ideas come from controlled chaos, and we are very good at controlled chaos. This is where experience makes way to intuition, and where truly original and compelling ideas are born. And if your feet are cold that’s because your socks just went missing again.

We Execute. We build the product/brand in a way that makes it stand out. The brand has something to say and we help it say it loud. Next thing you know–BAM! Where’d your socks go?
